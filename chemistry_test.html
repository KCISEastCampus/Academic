Moles Formula

This is the problematic one:

$$\text{moles} = \frac{\text{mass (g)}}{\text{molar mass (g/mol)}}$$

Energy Formula

$$Q = mc\Delta T$$

Where:

  • \(Q\) = Energy (unit: J)
  • \(m\) = Mass (unit: kg)
  • \(c\) = Specific heat capacity (unit: J/kg°C)
  • \(\Delta T\) = Temperature change (unit: °C)
