# A-level学生会首次系列娱乐活动成功举办 凝心聚力共迎大考

为帮助全体同学缓解大考前的紧张压力，丰富课余生活，增进彼此间的交流与友谊，**A-level学生会于2026年4月17日在教三四楼成功举办系列趣味娱乐活动**。本次活动是学生会成立运营以来的首次正式活动，在全体学生会成员的精心筹备与通力协作下，活动现场氛围热烈、秩序井然，获得了参与同学的一致好评。

本次活动精心设计了**你比划我来猜、青团连连看、不要做挑战、蒙眼尖叫鸡、谎言同盟**五大趣味游戏，覆盖不同互动形式，兼顾趣味性与参与性，充分调动了同学们的积极性。

在**你比划我来猜**游戏中，同学们用肢体动作演绎成语与趣味词汇，默契配合、妙趣横生，现场笑声不断；**青团连连看**以沙包投掷九宫格的形式展开比拼，团队协作的乐趣让同学们快速拉近了距离；**不要做挑战**凭借趣味禁言、禁动作规则，上演了一幕幕逗趣的“极限拉扯”，成为现场人气颇高的项目；**蒙眼尖叫鸡**依靠声音辨位的玩法紧张刺激，沉浸式互动让大家暂时忘却备考压力；**谎言同盟**则以主题演绎、真假猜测的形式，考验同学们的表达与推理能力，尽显思维与口才的魅力。

活动筹备期间，学生会各岗位工作人员各司其职、认真负责，从游戏设计、道具准备到现场组织、秩序维护，每一个环节都细致到位，保障了活动的顺利开展。参与同学们全身心投入游戏，在轻松欢乐的氛围中释放压力、交流互动，不仅收获了快乐，更加深了彼此的友谊，增强了集体凝聚力。

# A-level Student Council's First Series of Fun Events Successfully Held, Uniting Everyone for the Upcoming Official Exams

To help all students relieve pre-exam stress, enrich extracurricular life, and enhance communication and friendship among each other, the **A-level Student Council successfully held a series of fun events on April 17, 2026, in the fourth floors of the academic building 3**. This was the first official event since the establishment of the Student Council. With meticulous preparation and close collaboration among all members, the atmosphere at the event was lively and orderly, receiving unanimous praise from participating students.

Five creative and interactive games were carefully prepared for the event: **Charades, Qingtuan Connect, Don’t Do It Challenge, Blindfolded Squawk Chicken, and Lie Alliance**. Each game was designed to be lively, inclusive and engaging, drawing enthusiastic participation from all students.

In **Charades**, students paired up to act out words and idioms using only body language and facial expressions, with hilarious and heartwarming moments filling the venue. **Qingtuan Connect** was a team-based sandbag-tossing game that encouraged cooperation and quick thinking. The **Don’t Do It Challenge** tested students’ self-control with funny forbidden actions and phrases, becoming one of the most popular highlights of the day.

**Blindfolded Squawk Chicken** brought exciting and immersive fun: one blindfolded player tracked down peers with squawking chickens tied to their ankles using only sound, letting everyone temporarily forget exam pressure. **Lie Alliance** combined storytelling and guessing, challenging students’ expression, judgment and wit in a lighthearted way.

Throughout the event, all Student Council staff performed their duties earnestly—from game design and prop preparation to on-site coordination and order management, ensuring the event ran smoothly and safely. Students fully immersed themselves in the games, laughed freely, communicated openly, and strengthened bonds with one another in a relaxing atmosphere.

The success of this activity marks a solid start for the A-Level Student Council in serving fellow students and building a warm, united campus community. Moving forward, the A-Level Student Council will continue to organize diverse and meaningful events, help students balance study and leisure, and foster a positive, supportive and friendly campus culture. We wish every student face the upcoming exams with confidence and a calm mind!
