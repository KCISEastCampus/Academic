# 全新Logo发布

![Logo IMG](/assets/img/logo.png)

A-level学生会今日正式发布全新视觉标识（Logo）。新Logo以A-level课程核心特质为设计基底，精准融合组织使命与学子愿景，标志着学生会服务体系与品牌建设迈入全新阶段。

## 一、核心设计理念
全新Logo以**盾牌和书本**为核心视觉架构，承载双重核心寓意：
- 盾牌象征责任与守护，代表学生会始终以学子权益为核心，做学术成长与校园生活的护航者；
- 书本锚定A-level学术属性，寓意学生会以学术为基石，全力托举学子冲刺世界名校的梦想。

## 二、核心元素官方释义
### 1. 左上象限：书本与五星
星星对应A-level成绩体系中的**A\***等级，与翻开的书本结合：
- 寓意全体学子以极致追求冲刺A\*顶尖成绩，践行学术卓越目标；
- 彰显学生会助力学子斩获高分的服务宗旨。

### 2. 核心符号：字母“A”
盾牌右下象限的大写“A”，既是A-level课程标识，更代表三重内涵：
- **Ace（顶尖）**：呼应学子冲刺高分、追求卓越的核心目标；
- **Ambition（志向）**：鼓励学子树立远大理想，奔赴全球名校舞台；
- **All for Students（全心为学子）**：明确学生会一切服务的根本初心。

### 3. 其他元素
- 斜向条纹：体现A-level课程国际化、多元化特质，寓意学子锐意进取的成长姿态；
- 藏青/黑色主色调：象征学术严谨与组织公信力，契合A-level教育专业属性。

## 三、全新征程与应用
全新Logo将会应用于学生会官方宣传、活动物料和线上平台，作为组织专属视觉标识。未来，A-level学生会将持续优化各项核心工作，陪伴每一位学子在学术道路上砥砺前行，助力大家叩开世界名校大门，绽放青春光彩！

# New Logo Announcement

![Logo IMG](/assets/img/logo.png)

A-level Student Council announced the launch of our new logo. The new logo is designed based on the core characteristics of the A-level curriculum, precisely integrating the mission of the organization and the aspirations of the students. This marks a new stage in our service system and brand development.

## 1. Core Design Concept
The new logo is centered around the **shield & book** visual structure, carrying dual core meanings:
- The shield symbolizes responsibility and protection, representing the student council's commitment to prioritizing student rights and serving as a guardian of academic growth and campus life;
- The book anchors the academic nature of the A-level curriculum, symbolizing the student council's commitment to supporting students in pursuing their dreams of attending world-renowned universities.

## 2. Official Interpretation of Core Elements
### 1. Upper Left Quadrant: Book and Star

The star precisely corresponds to the **A\*** grade in the A-level grading system, combined with the open book:
- It signifies all students striving for the A\* top grade, embodying the goal of academic excellence;
- It highlights the student council's mission to help students achieve high scores and stand out in the international university admission process.

### 2. Core Symbol: Letter "A"
The capital "A" in the lower right quadrant of the shield represents three layers of meaning:
- **Ace**: Reflecting students' core goal of striving for high scores and pursuing excellence;
- **Ambition**: Encouraging students to set lofty ideals and aim for the global university stage;
- **All for Students**: Clarifying that the fundamental purpose of all student council services is to serve the students.

### 3. Other Elements
- Diagonal stripes: Reflecting the international and diverse characteristics of the A-level curriculum, symbolizing students' enterprising growth posture;
- Navy blue/black primary color: Symbolizing academic rigor and organizational credibility, fitting the professional attributes of A-level education.

## 3. New Journey and Application
The new logo will be comprehensively applied to the student council's official promotions, event materials and online platforms as the organization's exclusive visual identity. In the future, A-level Student Council will continue to optimize our core work and accompany every student on their academic journey, helping everyone open the doors to world-renowned universities and shine with youthful brilliance!
